Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

What areas do you serve?

We currently serve all five boroughs of New York City (Manhattan, Brooklyn, Queens, The Bronx, and Staten Island) as well as parts of Westchester County. Contact us to confirm if your location is within our service area.

How soon can you start a project?

For small projects and maintenance services, we can typically schedule within 1-2 weeks. For larger landscape design projects, the timeline depends on the complexity of the project and our current schedule. During peak seasons (spring and fall), wait times may be longer.

Do you offer free estimates?

Yes, we provide free estimates for all our services. For garden maintenance and other routine services, we can often provide estimates over the phone or via email. For landscape design and installation projects, we typically schedule an on-site consultation.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, checks, and all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover). For larger projects, we may require a deposit with the balance due upon completion.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes, Vinebud Gardening Services is fully licensed and insured in New York State. We carry general liability insurance and workers' compensation coverage. Certificates of insurance can be provided upon request.
